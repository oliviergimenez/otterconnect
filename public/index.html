OtterConnect

An applied research project to better understand otter recolonization

In the OtterConnect project, we aim at providing an operational framework for assessing socio-ecological connectivity for small carnivores which influence the dynamics of ecosystems and provide numerous ecosystem services. We focus on the Eurasian otter (Lutra lutra) an umbrella and indicator species of blue and green infrastructures.

Monitoring

How to survey efficiently otter populations to quantify habitat connectivity and suitability?

Modelling

What are the ecological conditions for otters to disperse and settle in areas or move between populations?

Interacting

How do human attitudes and representations shape habitat connectivity and suitability for otters?
