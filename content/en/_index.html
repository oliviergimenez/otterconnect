{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="white" %}} In the `OtterConnect` project, we aim at providing an operational framework for assessing socio-ecological connectivity for small carnivores which influence the dynamics of ecosystems and provide numerous ecosystem services.
