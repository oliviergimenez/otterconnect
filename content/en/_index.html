An applied research project to better understand otter recolonization

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="white" %}} In the `OtterConnect` project, we aim at providing an operational framework for assessing socio-ecological connectivity for small carnivores which influence the dynamics of ecosystems and provide numerous ecosystem services. We focus on the Eurasian otter (*Lutra lutra*) an umbrella and indicator species of blue and green infrastructures. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section color="dark" >}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-search" title="Monitoring"%}} How to survey efficiently otter populations to quantify habitat connectivity and suitability? {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-laptop" title="Modelling"%}} What are the ecological conditions for otters to disperse and settle in areas or move between populations? {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-users" title="Interacting"%}} How do human attitudes and representations shape habitat connectivity and suitability for otters? {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}}
